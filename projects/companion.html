Just One A Podcast

An android app that helped avoid roadblocks in hilly areas of India like Sikkim using crowsoucred real-time data. Partnered with Border Road Organisation (BRO) for providing verified alerts and helping authorities to clear raodblocks faster. The client APP was built on JAVA and Android MVC framework.

Consider Google Map but for hilly areas only and with real-time updates on roadblocks in areas with low to no-internet connectivity. The alerts were available and users could report roadblocks if they a mobile network in their devices, even without internet. Google Maps does not provide traffic alerts in hilly areas of India. This traffic alert feature even in plain/plateau areas was not readily available in India at the time.

APP was launched by Honorable Governor of Sikkim and this was my first startup.
